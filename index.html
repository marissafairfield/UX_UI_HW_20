Farming

The farm is overgrown and abandoned and needs a lot of work! You are able to get ahold of some seeds and a watering can, and get to work growing crops. As you explore the town, you find new types of seeds and crops that you can build your experience with. You also improve your watering capabilities so that you can grow even more crops. You find out you can raise different animals and sell their products - Cows, goats, pigs, chickens, dugs, and even some kind of dinosaur.

Combat

To craft necessities, you need to get ahold of different types of resources like wood, stone, and iron. You find out that the mines near town are filled with these types of resources, but also filled with monsters that you will have to fight in order to gather what you need. You find new types of weapons and build up your combat skills gradually.

Friendship

There are many neighbors around with different personalities, likes, and dislikes. You learn what each neighbor likes and start giving them presents to improve your friendship with them. Eventually you can date and marry a fellow villager and have a family.
